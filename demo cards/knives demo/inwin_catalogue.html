Master Craftsman

Kitchenware

INWIN was established in 1979, located in the hardware base of Yangjiang city. It is a private high-tech enterprise specializing in the professional manufacture of stainless steel knives, scissors, cutlery, and precision casting — for both export and domestic markets.

Guangdong INWIN Industrial Group Co., Ltd. operates multiple production bases: knives & scissors, precision casting, and kitchenware. Over 100,000 m² of standard workshops, more than 1,000 employees.
