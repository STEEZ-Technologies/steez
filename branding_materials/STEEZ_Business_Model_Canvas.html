Block 03  ·  Core
Value Propositions
One QR Unlimited Languages Zero Reprints Live Updates
  • Replace print entirely — one QR code replaces 500 catalogues, hundreds of business cards, and static signage
  • Instant multilingual — visitors self-select their language on arrival; unlimited languages supported
  • Always current — update product info, pricing, or contacts in seconds; the QR never changes
  • Digital business card — QR-linked microsite with company photos, map, WeChat/QQ links, export infographic & bilingual contact info
  • Interactive product catalogue — 3D model viewer (.glb/.gltf) for Premium clients; no AR complexity
  • QR Display Package — physical neck badges, desk stands & fullscreen display graphics for the expo floor
  • Dark/Light mode — adapts to any display environment

Primary model
factory → buyer
